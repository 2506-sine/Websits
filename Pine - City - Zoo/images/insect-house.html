INSECT HOUSE

The insect house is a fascinating place to explore the world of insects. It is home to a wide variety of species, including butterflies, beetles, and spiders. The insect house is designed to provide visitors with an up-close look at these amazing creatures, allowing them to learn about their habitats, behaviors, and life cycles. The insect house is also a great place for children to learn about the importance of insects in our ecosystem.

In addition to the live exhibits, the insect house also features interactive displays and educational materials that provide information about the different types of insects and their roles in nature. Visitors can learn about the life cycle of butterflies, the importance of bees in pollination, and the role of insects in decomposition. The insect house is a great place for families to spend time together while learning about the natural world.
