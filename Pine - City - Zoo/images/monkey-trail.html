MONKEY TRAIL

The monkey trail is a fun and exciting way to explore the zoo. It is a winding path that takes visitors through the heart of the zoo, allowing them to see a variety of animals up close. The trail is designed to mimic the natural habitats of the animals, providing a unique and immersive experience for visitors. Along the way, there are plenty of opportunities to stop and take photos, as well as educational displays that provide information about the animals and their habitats.

The monkey trail is suitable for all ages and fitness levels, making it a great activity for families and groups. The trail is well-maintained and easy to navigate, with plenty of benches and shaded areas for resting along the way. Visitors can also enjoy the beautiful scenery of the zoo, with lush gardens and stunning views of the surrounding area.
